[Sơ đồ 3: Quy trình từ lúc tạo Asset đến khi Unit hoạt động]
-------------------------------------------------------------
sequenceDiagram
    participant Designer as Designer (Asset Creation)
    participant Spawner as Castle/Spawner
    participant Controller as BaseUnitController
    
    Designer->>Spawner: Assigns UnitData Asset to Spawner
    Spawner->>Controller: Instantiate Prefab
    Spawner->>Controller: Init(data)
    Controller->>Controller: Read speed from data
    Controller->>Controller: Read health & attack from data
    Controller->>Controller: Use data values for runtime stats
    Controller-->>Controller: Ready for Gameplay
